Graphic & product designer with ~10 years of experience building complete brand identities — from the first concept to every surface the brand lives on.

I hold a B.Sc. in Industrial Design and combine that structural, system-level thinking with self-taught graphic craft. Most of my career has been freelance — studying a brief, designing the mark, and then building every touchpoint around it: stationery, signage, apparel, digital. The four projects on this site are my highest-level work, each one a full identity system, not just a logo. I also take on product design projects, drawing directly on my industrial design background and 3D software skills.
